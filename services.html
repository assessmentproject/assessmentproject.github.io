Services offered by Vahoghana

To improve the health and well-being of individuals and communities, here are some of the services we offer.

Clinical Laboratory Services

Clinical Laboratory Services play a vital role in healthcare by conducting diagnostic tests and analyses on patient samples. These services encompass a broad range of medical tests, from blood and urine analyses to genetic testing, aiding healthcare professionals in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.
